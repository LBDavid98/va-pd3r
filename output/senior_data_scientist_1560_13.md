# Position Description

**Position Title:** Senior Data Scientist
**Series/Grade:** 1560-13
**Reports To:** Chief Data Officer
**Supervisory Status:** Non-Supervisory

---

## Introduction

The Senior Data Scientist, GS-1560-13, is assigned to the organization and serves under the direction of the Chief Data Officer. This position provides senior-level technical leadership in advancing enterprise data analytics and data science capabilities to support mission priorities, improve operational effectiveness, and enable evidence-based decision-making across the organization. The incumbent leads enterprise analytics initiatives, develops and deploys machine learning models and related analytical solutions, and synthesizes complex data into actionable insights and recommendations for senior leadership. The work requires close collaboration with functional stakeholders and technical teams to ensure analytic products are timely, accurate, and aligned with organizational objectives and governance standards.
